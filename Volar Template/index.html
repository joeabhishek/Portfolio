Who Am I

Born in the city of destiny, Vizag, I am currently pursuing my Masters in Human Computer Interaction(HCI) in Indianapolis. I strongly believe in user centered design and development.

Apart from users and pixels, music and photography take a good chunk of my time.

What I Do

UX Research

I find out what users need through contextual inquiry, surveys, focus groups and domain research.

Interaction Design

Use the results from user research to design interactive digital products. Tools - Sketch App, Illustrator, PSD.

Prototyping

I prototype in 2 ways :
Framer.js, Invision
Rapid prototyping on web and mobile.

Web Development

I build great looking UI’s for the web using HTML5, CSS and Javascript. My server side armour includes Python, Java & PHP.

Mobile Development

I build iOS and Android apps.

Accessibility

I work on accessibility issues on both desktop and mobile for Blind and Visually Impaired(BVI) users.
